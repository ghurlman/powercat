About the Webinar Series

Microsoft Power CAT AI Webinars are a series of interactive virtual sessions designed to help your organization adopt Microsoft Copilot Studio and agents.

In these sessions you'll engage with Microsoft experts and other customers to gain the knowledge and skills needed to successfully adopt and thrive with agents in your organization.

The webinars challenge participants to devise strategies to accommodate AI, identify processes that can be enhanced or replaced, and focus on innovations that maximize ROI. We'll demonstrate how to identify and mitigate risks associated with generative AI and discuss new governance frameworks necessary for adopting agents responsibly, ensuring systemic and sustained value within an organization.

The sessions will be tailored for the needs of specific roles like executive sponsors, enterprise architects, data analysts, product owners, and administrators.

Covering value, governance, data foundations, and more, join one session or stay with us for the whole series to maximize your learning.
